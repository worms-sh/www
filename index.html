What is this?

It's very much a WIP :)

worms.sh is a web project and pubnix server* in the spirit of tilde.club and the small internet. This project is in its very early stages and is evolving, but here are some thoughts on current goals:

  1. Create a space to learn basic UNIX and web development
  2. Cultivate a creative community around collaborative web projects
  3. Foster exploration in a safe digital space

*not quite public (yet)
